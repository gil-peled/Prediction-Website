Links to other calculators

External liver-disease and hepatology calculators that may be useful in clinical practice. These are maintained by third parties.

  • Korean Association for the Study of the Liver (KASL) – Liver Disease Calculator

    A broad collection of liver calculators including Child-Pugh, MELD, MELD-Na, MELD 3.0, post-operative mortality risk, EASL CLIF-C ACLF, Maddrey discriminant function, Lille model, FIB-4, APRI, NAFLD fibrosis score, and others for fibrosis, alcoholic hepatitis, autoimmune hepatitis, and drug-induced liver injury.

  • MDCalc – MELD Score (Model for End-Stage Liver Disease)

    Widely used MELD and MELD-Na calculators for patients aged 12 and older, based on bilirubin, INR, creatinine, and sodium, used for transplant listing and prognosis in cirrhosis.

  • Hepatitis C Online (University of Washington) – FIB-4 Calculator

    Fibrosis-4 (FIB-4) calculator to estimate liver fibrosis using age, AST, ALT, and platelet count; includes interpretation and references. Part of the Hepatitis C Online curriculum.

  • MedCentral – Hepatology Calculators

    Collection of hepatology-related clinical calculators for liver disease assessment and management.

  • EF CLIF – CLIF-C AD Score Calculator

    CLIF-C Acute Decompensation (AD) score for predicting prognosis in hospitalized patients with acute decompensation of cirrhosis who do not have acute-on-chronic liver failure (ACLF). Uses age, creatinine, INR, white blood cell count, and sodium to estimate 1-, 3-, 6-, and 12-month mortality.
